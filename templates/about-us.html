About Us

OUR VISION, OUR MISSION!

MINEFLUX is a web-based application designed for Indian coal mines to quantify their carbon footprint and identify strategies for carbon neutrality. It addresses India's challenge of balancing coal dependency with climate change commitments. The application facilitates activity-specific emission calculations, evaluates existing carbon sinks, performs gap analyses, and suggests practical pathways towards carbon neutrality.

MINEFLUX Solution Overview:
• Emission estimation and per capita emission calculation.
• Simulation of emission reduction strategies including clean technologies, afforestation offsets, and renewable energy exploration.
• Carbon credit estimations, robust data visualization, and scalability for various mine types.
• Aims for transparency, decision support, sustainability, and cost efficiency.
